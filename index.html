How to Use CyberGrader.io

Three blocks at the top:

  • Teacher Feedback — pre-written gradebook feedback for assignments, projects, and welcome messages.
  • Email Generator — bulk or single-student emails for check-ins, reminders, and end-of-term communication.
  • SP Portal Setup — exact text to paste into the portal gradebook.

Workflow: Pick a block, narrow down the type, review the generated text, click Edit to customize, then Save Globally to lock your version. Reset Template wipes your edits. Copy when ready.

School year dates are set once at sign-in and sync across devices. Edit them anytime from the user menu (top right).
