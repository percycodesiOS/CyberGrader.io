How to Use

  • Pick the tab you need: Grading, Email, or Library.
  • Click Copy. Paste into the destination.
  • Edit lets you change wording. Save Override locks your edit on this device.
  • Reset returns to the original locked wording.

4th Nine Weeks

When Term is set to Fourth Nine Weeks, the email generator automatically switches the check-in templates to the dual-deadline format that lists both Grades 7–11 and Senior deadlines in the same message.

Sync Across Devices

Move your saved overrides between devices.

Communication Flow

  • Welcome at course start
  • Early check-in after add/drop
  • 25% / 50% / 75% progress reminders
  • Next nine weeks loaded notice
  • Spring break / final week reminders
  • End-of-year closeout
