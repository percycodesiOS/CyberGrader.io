How to Use

  • Set up the year once in Year Setup — all deadlines autofill after that.
  • Pick the tab you need, click copy, paste into the destination.
  • Edit lets you change wording.
  • Save Override locks your edits on this device.
  • Reset returns the original locked wording — only for the current selection.

Cyber Flow

Beginning of year

  • 1. Complete Year Setup with all deadline dates.
  • 2. Set up Portal Information (announcements + grade setup).
  • 3. Send the Welcome Email.
  • 4. Begin grading first assignments.

Throughout each nine weeks

  • Early check-in after add/drop settles.
  • 25%, 50%, and 75% progress reminders.
  • Next nine weeks loaded notice before rollover.
  • Final week reminder + closeout.

Portal Information

Weekly Overall Grade Update

Assignment Name: Weekly Overall Grade Update

This grade reflects the student's current weekly progress for the grading period and is updated weekly. Individual assignment and project grades are available in Microsoft Teams and/or Carnegie Learning platform(s).

Final Overall Grade

Assignment Name: Final Overall Grade

This grade reflects the student's overall average for the nine-week grading period. Individual assignment and project grades can be viewed in Microsoft Teams and/or Carnegie Learning platform(s).

End of Period Reminder

After final grades are entered at the end of the nine weeks, remove the Weekly Overall Grade Update and keep only the Final Overall Grade.
