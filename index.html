How to Use

  • Set up the year once in Year Setup. All deadlines auto-fill after that.
  • Pick the tab you need. Click Copy. Paste into the destination.
  • Edit lets you change wording. Save Override locks your edit on this device.
  • Reset returns to the original locked wording.
