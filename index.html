How to Use

  • Grading Output is for entering student feedback into the gradebook.
  • Email Generator is for consistent, recurring student communication.
  • Copy first, then make only the smallest custom edit needed.
  • Edit Output → button switches to Save → click Save to lock it in globally.
  • Reset Template wipes your saved override and restores the original.

Grading Period Schedule

Nine Weeks 1–3

All students (grades 7–12) start and end on the same schedule for the first, second, and third nine weeks. Deadlines and pacing apply equally across grade levels.

Fourth Nine Weeks — Split Deadlines

  • Seniors: earlier deadline — no extensions. Directly affects graduation status.
  • Grades 7–11: standard end-of-year deadline.
